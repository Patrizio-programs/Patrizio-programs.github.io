Site Purpose

This site is for the purpose of educating and advising people (those 14 and up) about the weather on a daily basis This website will provide the users with accurate and up to date information of the forecast for the next five days. It will also have detaied information on storms passing through or around the area we cover. (Preston Idaho) The site will have a friendly light look and be easy to manuever and understand so that older residents will be able to use the site with ease.
