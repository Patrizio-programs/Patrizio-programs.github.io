Rental Agreement

Please note that once you rent one of our vehicles you agree to all aspects of the below rental agreement. Reservations must be made at least 8 hours before pickup.


1. All scooters would be rented with full tanks of gas, and must be returned at a full tank of gas
2. A security deposit of $200 dollars is required for all rentals. Granted that there are no damages upon return of the scooter, the deposit will be returned
3. All scooters should be returned no later then 6:00 p.m. on the agreed day of return
4. Only the individual who signed that rental agreement should be driving the scooter
5. Scooters should not be taken out of state. If so, legal actions will be taken.
