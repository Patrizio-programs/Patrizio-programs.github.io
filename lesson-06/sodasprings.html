Soda Springs, Idaho - Geyser Park

Truly a sight to behold. If geysers and springs are your thing then Soda Springs is the place to be! There are three springs you can find here and they are all worth a visit! Hooper Springs, Octagon Springs and Lover's Delight. Don't waste anymore time and come see the geyser in full view as well as check out the natural hot springs.
